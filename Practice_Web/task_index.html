About Roshni Public School

Founded in 2020, Roshni Public School has been a beacon of quality education in Sakandri, Pakistan. Affiliated with CBSE, we combine modern teaching methods with strong moral values to prepare students for life's challenges.

Our experienced faculty, well-equipped classrooms, and nurturing environment create the perfect space for every child to grow — academically, socially, and spiritually.
